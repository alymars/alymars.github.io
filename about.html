My Story

I have worked in retail, property management, banking, parks and recreation, and tech. Despite being vastly different fields, there was a common theme; customers want a good experience. It makes no difference if they're buying bed sheets, renting an apartment, or using an app. They want to enjoy doing it and feel elevated by the end of their journey. I've been able to transfer my previous skills and experiences into my designs, making them intuitive and elegant. I can accomplish this by making sure I understand the full scope of the problem and conducting thorough research.
