My Story

I have worked in retail, property management, banking, parks & recreation, and, most recently, SaaS. These industries have a common theme: people want a good experience. It makes no difference if they buy bed sheets, rent an apartment, or use an app. People want to feel like their needs are being met and that there is value in their interactions.
My commitment to customer-first thinking sparked my interest in UX. I have developed a deep empathy for the user and learned firsthand their pain points. My mission is to deliver clear and concise solutions that ensure success for both users and stakeholders.
I am always looking for new challenges and opportunities to grow, and I am excited to continue my journey into UX and grow as a professional.
